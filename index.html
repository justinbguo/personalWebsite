Justin Guo -


I'm Justin - a junior at the University of Michigan studying business with minors in computer science & entrepreneurship. Hope you're doing well!







Professionally, I want to pursue product at a consumer-facing tech company and eventually commit to pursuing my own entrepreneurial venture. Positive, tangible impact -the kind where you can immediately observe your decisions making people feel a little better- always brings a smile to my face, and I truly believe that there is no better place to see that impact than the technology we use every single day.


Right now, I'm building a mobile app with a couple of friends to help college students form stronger friendships through more spontaneous hangouts. I also work as a manager for an independent music artist (Habe) who makes some lovely tunes. In the past, I've worked as a PM intern at a consumer social app startup, founded a YouTube channel, and ran an e-commerce store selling electric massage balls, where I've honed strengths like data analysis, UI/UX mastery, and working with diverse teams.


Empathy, imagination, playfulness, passion, authenticity, and curiosity are the values I hold most strongly. If you're interested, you can read more about me here.
